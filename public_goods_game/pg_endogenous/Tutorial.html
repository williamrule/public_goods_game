You will play {{ C.NUM_ROUNDS }} rounds. In each round, you begin with a firm formation stage. Unlike Treatments 1 and 2, firms are endogenously formed: each participant can potentially own a firm, apply to other firms, and be accepted or rejected.

Firm formation

  • You begin each round as the potential owner of your own firm.
  • You may apply to another participant’s firm by clicking their firm icon.
  • If your application is accepted, that acceptance is binding for the round.
  • If you are accepted into another firm, your own firm becomes inactive for that round.
  • If you accept an applicant into your firm, you are committed to operating that firm and may no longer apply elsewhere in that round.
  • Applications that are still pending when firm formation ends are automatically rejected.
  • Firm size is capped at {{ C.MAX_FIRM_SIZE }}.

Autarky

If you are not employed by another firm and no one joins your own firm, you remain alone (autarky) for that round. In autarky, you simply keep your {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} effort units and earn {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} points.

Your decision each round

If you are in a firm with other members, you receive {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} effort units and choose how many units to put into the firm. The rest you keep for yourself.

  • Effort kept for yourself earns 1 point per unit.
  • Firm effort is added up across all members of the firm.
  • The firm’s payoff is shared equally among firm members, including you.
  • Your individual contribution is not directly shown to other players.

How firm payoffs work

In some sessions, payoffs use the constant-return schedule from the exogenous treatments:

In other sessions, the firm payoff has increasing returns to scale within the firm: higher total firm effort leads to disproportionately higher total firm output, which is then shared equally.

Resume information

Every participant has a public resume showing their prior firm history, including:

  • which firm they worked in,
  • the members of that firm,
  • firm size,
  • per-capita effort,
  • per-capita payout,
  • and whether they were terminated by a prior employer.

Timing

  • You have {{ C.FORMATION_SECONDS }} seconds for firm formation.
  • You then have {{ C.DECISION_SECONDS }} seconds to choose your effort allocation.
  • If time runs out on the decision page, your firm contribution is set to 0.
  • After decisions are made, there is a {{ C.INFO_SECONDS }}-second information screen.

Payments

You earn points during the experiment. Points are converted to cash at the end.
